PANABO CITY SEAWALL

The Panabo City Seawall is one of the go-to places of Panaboans as a place of solemn refuge or simply a place to enjoy time with family and friends. Though Panabo City is not that grand as to its neighboring cities with a lot of building and infrastructures towering over people, it has given importance to its natural sceneries, not just as a source of livelihood but at the same time a spot for tourism, which are both in progress.
