BRGY. CAGANGOHAN

Barangay Cagangohan is located in the city of Panabo, Davao del Norte province in the Philippines. It is situated at approximately 7.2815° N, 125.6829° E, with an estimated elevation of 9.1 meters or 29.9 feet above sea level. According to the 2020 census, Barangay Cagangohan had a population of 13,224, representing 6.32% of Panabo's total population. In 2015, the barangay had 13,776 residents living in 3,100 households, averaging 4.44 members per household. The age group with the highest population in Cagangohan in 2015 was 5 to 9 years old, with 1,382 individuals, while the lowest population was in the 80 and over age group, with only 65 individuals.

The population of Barangay Cagangohan has been steadily increasing over the past 30 years. In 1990, the population was 5,229, which grew to 13,224 in 2020, an increase of 7,995 people. However, the latest census figures in 2020 show a negative growth rate of 0.86%, or a decrease of 552 people, from the previous population of 13,776 in 2015.

Barangay Cagangohan shares borders with the barangays of J.P. Laurel, Salvacion, San Vicente, New Pandan (Poblacion), and Vicente Hizon Sr., Davao City.
